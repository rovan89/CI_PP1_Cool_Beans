Our Mission

Our Mission

We strongly feel that a little can make a big difference, that’s why we are making it our priority to do the best we can for the world and everyone in it. For every cup of coffee we sell, we are giving 25% of our profits to developing countries who need our support, and another 25% to help tackle climate change. Sure what good is our world if we all don’t pitch in.
